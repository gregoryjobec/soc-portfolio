Completed the Wireshark module series on TryHackMe (The Basics, Packet Operations, Traffic Analysis), earning the Wireshark badge.

While most day-to-day triage happens at the Sentinel/Defender alert layer, packet-level analysis is the layer underneath every detection. This module reinforced my ability to validate alerts at the network level when deeper investigation is needed — for example confirming beaconing behavior or unusual protocol usage flagged by an automated detection.
